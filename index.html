Keyloggers: A Threat to Privacy [Mar 2022 - May 2022 (3 months)]

Research

I worked on Keyloggers and Touchloggers for a university project.
For the project, I was asked to selected a IEEE research paper on any topic as a base paper and then five more supporting paper on the same topic, which should expand the topic. They should also answer the questions from the base paper.
I searched for papers on Cybersecurity, but finding a paper on which I can find five more papers was a challenge.
Then I found this paper paper on keyloggers which was very interesting.
And there were also papers which expanded and answered the questions from the base paper.
